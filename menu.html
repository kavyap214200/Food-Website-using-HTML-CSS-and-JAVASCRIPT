Vegetarian Menu

Appetizers

  • Stuffed Mushrooms - Fresh mushrooms stuffed with a savory filling of breadcrumbs, cheese, and herbs.
  • Vegetable Spring Rolls - Crisp spring rolls filled with a mix of fresh vegetables and served with a tangy dipping sauce.
  • Caprese Skewers - Cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil leaves drizzled with balsamic glaze.

Main Courses

  • Paneer Tikka Masala - Grilled paneer cubes cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce, served with naan or rice.
  • Vegetable Biryani - Aromatic basmati rice cooked with a blend of spices and mixed vegetables, garnished with fresh coriander.
  • Stuffed Bell Peppers - Bell peppers filled with a mixture of rice, beans, and spices, baked to perfection.
  • Eggplant Parmesan - Breaded and fried eggplant slices layered with marinara sauce and melted cheese.
  • Chickpea Curry - A flavorful curry made with chickpeas simmered in a spiced coconut milk sauce.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Avocado Mousse - A creamy and rich mousse made with ripe avocados and cocoa powder, sweetened with agave syrup.
  • Fruit Salad - A refreshing mix of seasonal fruits drizzled with a light honey and mint dressing.
  • Carrot Halwa - A traditional Indian dessert made with grated carrots, milk, sugar, and ghee, garnished with nuts and raisins.
